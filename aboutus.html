About Mercury Trust

We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to providing exceptional Qualified Settlement Fund administration with integrity and precision.

Our Story

Learn about our journey to becoming a trusted leader in QSF administration.

Mercury Trust Company was founded by a team of financial and legal experts who recognized the need for specialized, client-focused Qualified Settlement Fund administration. Our founders brought together decades of combined experience in trust administration, financial management, and regulatory compliance.

Since our inception, we have been guided by a simple philosophy: to provide unparalleled service with unwavering integrity. We understand that behind every settlement fund are real people whose lives have been affected and who deserve the highest level of care and professionalism in the administration of their funds.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence by combining technical expertise with a personalized approach to client service. We have managed QSFs for settlements of all sizes across a diverse range of industries and case types, always maintaining our commitment to regulatory compliance and fiduciary responsibility.

Today, Mercury Trust Company stands as a trusted partner to law firms, corporations, and claimants nationwide, helping to ensure that settlement funds are managed efficiently, distributed accurately, and administered in full compliance with all applicable regulations.
