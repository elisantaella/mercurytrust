QSF Services Overview

Trusted Settlement Solutions. Human-Focused Impact.

We provide specialized Qualified Settlement Fund (QSF) administration services designed to support law firms, corporations, and courts with precise, secure, and compassionate post-settlement solutions. From release administration through to payment, we streamline every step of the disbursement process—maximizing efficiency while safeguarding the needs of claimants and the integrity of each settlement.

What We Do

Our focus is singular: we deliver end-to-end QSF administration tailored for complex settlements, including class actions, mass torts, and single-event resolutions.

By managing the flow of settlement funds with transparency and accountability, we help ensure timely and equitable outcomes for all stakeholders. With a combination of legal insight, operational precision, and cutting-edge technology, we offer clients a seamless experience grounded in trust and results.

Fund Establishment and Escrow Management

We handle all aspects of QSF creation, including court filings, tax identification, and secure account setup. Our team ensures proper fund structure and management from day one.

Release and Payment Administration

We efficiently process releases, verify eligibility criteria, and execute timely payments to claimants according to established protocols and settlement agreements.

Lien Resolution and Compliance Support

Our experts navigate the complexities of healthcare liens, government benefits, and other potential encumbrances to ensure proper resolution and compliance.

Tax Reporting and Accounting Services

We manage all tax filings, reporting requirements, and maintain comprehensive financial records throughout the settlement administration process.

Customized Claimant Communication Strategies

We develop and implement tailored communication plans to keep claimants informed and engaged throughout the settlement process.

Data-Driven Reporting and Analytics

Our advanced reporting tools provide stakeholders with real-time insights into fund activities, distributions, and overall settlement progress.
